Kinetic Installation

Compound pendulum

A compound pendulum driven by two motors and reacts to the users movements - creating a virtual drawing using light. The project combines the erratic movement of compound pendulums, with high-fidelity control of stepper motors.

London, UK

Prototype 2016

In collaboration with Sini (Nana) Bian, Xue (Lyn) Chen, Samerah Mohamand, Martin Stacey and Xinwei Zhuang. Tuturs: William Bondin, Francois Mangion, Chris Leung
