WhereIsMyTransport mapped and digitalised public transport in emerging markets where informal networks — minibuses, shared taxis, motorcycle taxis — make up 80% of all daily trips but have never appeared on a map. The company [raised $14.5m Series A](https://archive.md/rXXRp) and mapped 50 cities across Africa, Latin America, and South East Asia.

<Figure src="/images/projects/whereismytransport/hub.png" caption="Informal public transport hub in Kampala, Uganda" />


## Lead PM — Real-Time Alerts (B2B)

End-to-end ownership of the real-time public transport alerts data product. I established a cross-functional R&D team (data scientists, MLOps, backend) and led the transformation from manual monitoring to AI-augmented operations.

<Figure src="/images/projects/whereismytransport/architecture.png" size="medium" />

**Multi-stage LLM pipeline** — Designed a system to find, locate, and classify traffic alerts in noisy, unstructured social media feeds across multiple languages (including Thai, where tokenisation alone is a significant challenge). The pipeline supports or replaces human operators based on context and confidence.

**Result:** Full automation during costly night shifts while maintaining human oversight during peak hours. Annual operational costs reduced by over 25%.
<Figure src="/images/projects/whereismytransport/app-alerts.png" caption="Live Alerts"  size="small" />
