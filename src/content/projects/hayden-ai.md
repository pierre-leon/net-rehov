Hayden AI combines advanced vision AI with purpose-built, vehicle-mounted hardware to analyse urban environments in real time. Bus-mounted cameras detect parking violations, bus lane infractions, and double-parking - the everyday disruptions that make public transit unreliable.

## The Problem

Buses are late because cars are in the way. A parked vehicle in a bus stop or bus lane creates a cascade of delays affecting thousands of riders. Traditional enforcement is too slow and too sparse to change driver behaviour at scale.

## What Hayden Does

Vehicle-mounted cameras scan continuously, identifying violations automatically and generating evidential packages for enforcement. Deployed on buses across major US cities, the platform has improved on-time performance, reduced collisions along bus routes, and made streets more accessible - particularly for seniors and wheelchair users.
