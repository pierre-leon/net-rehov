Sownd is on a mission to transform how people experience sound in social spaces — turning noise into harmony through real-time acoustic monitoring and certification.

The core product is **Sownd Certification** — the world's first acoustic certification designed around real human experience rather than technical compliance metrics. It verifies that a space is genuinely comfortable for people with hearing differences, not just technically within spec.

## My Role

As Fractional Head of Product, I led product strategy and roadmap across the monitoring platform and certification product — working with the founder to structure the product organisation, define the B2B go-to-market, and prioritise the features that would drive venue adoption.
